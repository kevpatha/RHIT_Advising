# ME Course Planner — Student Guide

This tool helps you map out your four-year ME schedule at Rose-Hulman. You can either let it generate a suggested plan, or build your own quarter by quarter.

---

## Getting Started

**1. Pick your plan** using the dropdown at the top left:
- *Standard* — the default ME track
- *Calculus ABC* — if you're completing MA 113 the summer before Sophomore year
- *Rose² MEM or MME* — if you're pursuing a dual degree

**2. Set your starting year and quarter.** If you're already partway through the program, set this to where you currently are — courses you've already taken won't be rescheduled.

**3. Choose Vibrations or Controls** — this selects between EM 306 and ME 306 for your Junior year elective track.

**4. Mark any Pre-RHIT credits** if you arrived with AP, IB, or transfer credit for courses like Calc I or General Chemistry. Those courses will be treated as already completed.

---

## Two Ways to Plan

### Option A — Suggest a Plan

Click **Suggest plan →** and the tool will automatically build a four-year schedule for you, respecting:
- Course prerequisites and quarter availability
- Elective distribution targets (HSSA spread across all four years, technical electives held for Senior year, etc.)
- Your starting point and any courses already completed

You can then adjust the suggested schedule manually using the grid.

### Option B — Build Your Own

Use the course grid to drag elective slots into quarters, add or remove courses, and see your credit totals update in real time. Course cards will turn red if a prerequisite isn't met, so you can catch sequencing problems early.

---

## Reading the Schedule

- **Xcr / Ych** — credits vs. contact hours. Many lab and studio courses have more contact hours than their credit value suggests.
- **delayed** badge — the course is placed earlier than its standard year. This is allowed if prerequisites are met, but is flagged so you're aware.
- Red card — a prerequisite or availability issue exists for that placement.

---

## Elective Types

| Label | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| HSSA Elective | Humanities, Social Sciences & Arts — 7 slots total across four years |
| Technical Elective | ME-related elective — 4 slots, all in Senior year |
| Math/Science Elective | Quantitative elective — 1 slot |
| Free Elective | Any course — 2 slots, 1 in Junior and 1 in Senior year |

> HUM H190 (First-Year Writing Seminar) and ENGL H290 (Tech & Professional Communication) are required HSSA courses but are **separate** from your 7 HSSA elective slots — they don't count against that total.

---

## Exporting Your Schedule

When you're happy with your plan, click **⬇ Excel** to download a `.xlsx` file with your full schedule. This is a good way to save a snapshot or share it with your advisor.

---

## Tips

- If a quarter looks overloaded, try toggling **Show Summer** to see whether a Summer option opens up space.
- The tool assumes all electives are **4 credits** unless you manually adjust your completed credit counts on the left panel.
- Your plan updates live — there's no save button needed while you're working in the browser, but export to Excel if you want to keep a copy.
